The Process

From chile to bottle, four weeks.

Week 1

Mash

Fresh chiles are stemmed, salted, and packed into fermentation crocks the same day they're picked.

Week 2–3

Ferment

Wild lacto-fermentation builds tang and depth — we taste every batch to know when it's ready.

Week 4

Blend

Mashed chiles are blended with vinegar and fruit, then rested again to let the flavors settle.

Bottling day

Bottle

Every bottle is filled, labeled, and capped by hand in small runs — no large-batch shortcuts.
